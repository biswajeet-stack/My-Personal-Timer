A Note on Your Schedule's Foundation

This plan prioritizes getting at least 6 hours of sleep, a significant boost from 4. This change is the single most important factor for improving mental and physical performance. By using the early morning for deep work, you capitalize on your most productive, distraction-free hours, ensuring your growth activities aren't compromised by a long workday.
